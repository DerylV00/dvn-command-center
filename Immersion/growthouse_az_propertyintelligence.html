★ The Speakeasy — Most Important Room in the Property

The Speakeasy

Nobody expects this room. That is exactly why it works. Staircase access from the lower level, dark red velvet curtains, a Chesterfield leather sofa, barrel chairs, Persian rug, gold lighting, a bar in the corner. It is a fully designed speakeasy inside a mansion.

The Speakeasy · Lower Level
The Room Nobody Knew Existed.
For the 8-figure Day 3 Immersion circle — this is the room Jesse uses. Intimate. Dark. No pool visible. No distraction. The environment is designed for deep conversation. Ten people in this room with Jesse feels like being let into something that most people never see.

For cocktail night — this becomes the most talked-about moment of the event. During the first hour, Deryl does a curated reveal: "There is a speakeasy downstairs. I'm opening it at 8pm." Guests find it through the staircase. The red curtain parts. The room is already lit and a bartender is running a specialty whiskey and cigar service. Guests who discover it feel like they've found something. That feeling is impossible to manufacture — but this room already has it built in.

How It Gets Used

★Day 3 Immersion Day: Jesse leads the 8-figure inner circle in this room. The environment matches the energy — closed, intimate, no performative element. The real conversations happen in rooms like this.
★Cocktail Night — Timed Reveal: Deryl announces the speakeasy opens at 8pm. One bartender running whiskey and cigar service. Maximum 12–15 people in the room at once. Floor staff directs guests who seem to want a quieter moment.
★Late-night closing circle: After the main cocktail night winds down, Jesse can pull the inner circle down here for the final conversation of the weekend. The room is already perfect for it.
★Amys moment: The reveal shot — red curtain parting, amber light, leather chairs, a group walking into the room for the first time. This is a hero content moment.
✓ Nobody in the mastermind space has a speakeasy in their venue. This is Growth House's unfair advantage. Use it deliberately.

Wellness Suite

The Wellness Suite

This property has a commercial-grade wellness facility. The am-Finn dry sauna, a full steam room, the pool and spa, and a master soaking tub. This is not a nice-to-have. It is a differentiator that should be communicated to members before they arrive.

Wellness Suite · Lower Level + Pool
Pre-heat. Pre-stage. Ready before anyone wakes.

The wellness window runs 7–9am every immersion day. Sessions start at 10am. The gap is intentional — it is unstructured networking time, recovery time, and mental preparation time. The wellness suite is not the agenda. It is the environment for conversations that can't happen in a session room.
