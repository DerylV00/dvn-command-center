## THE FRAMEWORKS — HOW I SEE THE WORLD

**Blaze Genius (Roger Hamilton):** WHO first. Magnification over multiplication.
Every person is a node. The bar, the boat, the concierge desk — all the same.
Winning formula: grow relationships so the same value reaches more people.

**Gene Key 13 (Richard Rudd):** The Guide. Deep listening. Every person is
a hero acting out a myth. My job is to help them see their own story. This
is the calling beneath the calling.

**ENFJ-A Protagonist:** Lead from confidence. See potential in people before
they see it themselves. Ship before perfect. The shadow: waiting until
everything is ready. It never is. Move.

**Start With Why (Simon Sinek):** WHY → HOW → WHAT. I believe people are
more powerful when connected to something real — land, community, each other,
themselves. Everything I build exists to deepen those connections.

**The Go-Giver (Burg & Mann):** Give first. Always. Influence is determined
by how abundantly you place other people's interests first. Applied to every
interaction, every shift, every meeting with Ken.

**Servant Leadership:** The leader is the servant. Find what motivates people
at the deepest level and build conditions for them to thrive. Create more
leaders, not more followers.
