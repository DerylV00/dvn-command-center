The Format

Four Days. One Weekend.

The Growth House weekend runs Thursday through Sunday. Each day has its own energy profile, team size, and operational requirements. Two of the four days are separate productions.

Thursday
Growth House Day
Full mastermind. Sessions, hot seats, deep work. Wellness window 7–9am. Active outdoor breaks between every session. Elevated 7-figure+ dinner closes the night. The linear fire runs. Full team.
Friday
Immersion Day
Inner circle only. 10–20 members. The speakeasy. Deep work in the room that demands real conversation. This day is an invitation — not a continuation of Thursday. Lean team.
★ Production 02
Saturday
Cocktail Night
Full standalone production. 6pm–10pm. DJ, security, three bars, photo booth, speakeasy reveal at 8pm, rooftop opens at 9pm. 13–16 staff. Every activation calculated.
Sunday
Fun Day
Off the property. ATVs, Navy SEALs, Sedona, jet skis — Jesse locks the activity. Physical, high-energy, bonding-focused. The barriers between members fully dissolve. Gas vehicles only.

The Principle

The North Star Filter.

One principle governs every production decision in this system. When a choice is unclear, run it through this filter and the answer becomes obvious.

"The environment should never be neutral."
Growth House Operations Principle · Derived from Zach Kepes · Arizona 2026
Every zone, every transition, every moment of the day should either be building energy, directing energy, or intentionally releasing it. Nothing passive. Nothing that just fills time. When the run of show serves the energy, the event works. When the energy serves the run of show, the event breaks.
