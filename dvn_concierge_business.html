  • Segment size: Large — hundreds of qualifying companies

Targeting Decision: DVN Concierge pursues Segment 1 exclusively for the first 18 months. One exceptional client experience in a tight community generates more pipeline value than any marketing spend. Segment 2 activates naturally through Growth House events. Segment 3 is a 3-year build.
